Our Story

For 50 years, our family has been handcrafting pure Pashmina in the heart of Kashmir.

What began as a small artisan workshop is now a proud legacy of patience, precision, and timeless beauty.

Our shawls, rumals, and masars are loved across the Gulf countries for their softness, purity, and elegant finish.

Today, we bring this heritage into the digital world—making authentic Kashmiri luxury accessible beyond borders.

No middlemen. No compromises. Just tradition, delivered directly to you.
